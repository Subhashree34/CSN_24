Blink-182

The Punk Rock Legends

Blink-182 is a legendary American rock band that formed in 1992. Known for their catchy pop-punk sound,
they gained massive popularity with hits like "All the Small Things" and "What's My Age Again?" The band,
consisting of Mark Hoppus, Tom DeLonge, and Travis Barker, became icons of the late 90s and early 2000s,
influencing a generation with their energetic music and relatable lyrics.
